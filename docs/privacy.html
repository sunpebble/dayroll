Privacy Policy

Dayroll — One Line a Day · Last updated 3 July 2026

Dayroll is a personal journaling app built to keep your writing entirely on your own device. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

Data we collect

None. Dayroll does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

Where your entries live

Everything you write — your daily lines, moods, and streaks — is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage. Your entries never leave your device unless you explicitly export or share them yourself using the system share sheet. If you have iCloud device backup enabled, your data is included in that backup under Apple's terms; we never have access to it.

Purchases

Dayroll offers an optional one-time in-app purchase (Dayroll Pro). Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Apple handles all payment information; Dayroll never sees or stores your payment details. We only receive Apple's confirmation that a purchase or restore succeeded, which unlocks the Pro features on your device.

Tracking & third parties

Dayroll contains no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. We do not use the Advertising Identifier and we do not track you across apps or websites.

Children

Dayroll does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
