DAYROLL

* ONE LINE A DAY *

Dayroll is a quiet daily journal. One line, one mood, every day — printed onto an endless paper tape you can scroll back through for years. No feeds, no accounts, no noise. Just today, and the streak you're keeping.

What it does

  • One line a day. A single thought, logged in seconds.
  • Moods & streaks. Tag how the day felt and watch your streak grow.
  • Stats (Pro). See your patterns — moods, consistency, months at a glance.
  • Export (Pro). Take your entries with you as plain text, anytime.
  • Home Screen widget. Today's line, always in reach.

Private by design

Everything stays on your device. Dayroll has no servers, no accounts, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. Your writing is yours alone. See our Privacy Policy.
